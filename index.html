FoBo - The Food Bot

Feeling hungary while enjoying movie? Don't want to leave your seat? Hate long queues in interval? Try FoBo.

FoBo takes your order with few clicks and delivered within 15 minutes.

Welcome to world of FoBo

No Need to download application

Open Facebook Messenger app and search for @FoBoai and start chatting with FoBo. FoBo will take your order on messenger.

Select Cinema

Click on Getting start button, Fobo will ask you to select Cinema hall.

Select what you want to order

Fobo will show all food menu from select Cinema. Select snacks that your want to order.

Place your Order

Click on checkout, Provide your Seat number and procced to the payment. Food will be delivered at your movie seat within 15 min.
